About Us

RE-volv is a nonprofit organization that helps 501(c)(3) nonprofits
install solar panels. RE-volv provides solar financing so that you can go
solar without having to pay anything upfront. We are committed to
helping those who help our community and helping organizations who
do important work focus on their goals while benefiting from solar.
RE-Volv empowers people and communities to invest collectively in
energy. We envision a world where people are thriving in communities
powered by clean energy.
